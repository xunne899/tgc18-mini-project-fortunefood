Agedashi Tofu

Agedashi Tofu

Serves: 6

Ingredients
  • 1 pack Fortune Chinese Tofu.
  • Cornstarch or flour.
  • Oil for deep frying.
Seasoning
  • ¼ cup light soy sauce.
  • ½ cup boiling water.
  • 2 tsp minced ginger.
  • Pinch of white pepper.
  • Sesame oil to taste.
    • * Combine all sauce ingredients. Set aside as dipping sauce.
Directions
  1. Cut tofu into 6 cubes.
  2. Roll the tofu cubes in cornstarch or flour until all sides are coated.
  3. Heat work and add oil. When the oil is heated sufficiently (~180ºC), carefully add the tofu cubes into the wok.
  4. Fry, stirring occasionally, until the tofu cubes are golden on all sides.
  5. Remove and drain oil on paper towels.
  6. Serve with Soy-Ginger Sauce.
